# Advanced Mode Feature Roadmap

Research from Edgewonk, TraderSync, TradesViz, and other trading journal platforms.

## Tier 1 — High Impact
- **Trade Replay/Simulator** — Replay past trades with market data to review decisions
- **Performance Simulator** — Monte Carlo projections of future P&L based on current stats
- **Automated Tagging** — AI auto-tags trades based on chart patterns, time of day, volatility
- **Multi-Timeframe Analysis** — Break down performance by session (Asia/EU/US), hour, day of week

## Tier 2 — Power User
- **Strategy Backtesting** — Test rule-based strategies against historical trade data
- **Custom Metrics Builder** — User-defined calculated columns (e.g., "avg hold time on winners")
- **Correlation Matrix** — How setups, emotions, and time of day correlate with outcomes
- **Risk Distribution Analysis** — Position size optimization, Kelly criterion calculations

## Tier 3 — Future
- **Exchange API Auto-Import** — Connect Binance, Bybit, etc. for automatic trade logging
- **Multi-Account Portfolio View** — Aggregate stats across multiple exchange accounts
- **Social/Community** — Share anonymized stats, compare with peer group
- **Trade Journaling Templates** — Pre-built review templates for different strategies
