The problem

You know you revenge trade. You just can’t see it happening in real time.

Stargate flags when you enter multiple trades after a loss, detects oversizing, and shows you the pattern before you blow your week.

Every trade gets a process score. Did you follow your entry criteria? Your position sizing? Your stop loss? Stargate tracks it so you can’t lie to yourself.
